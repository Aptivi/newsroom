Since March 1st, 2019, Aptivi Blog started as a tech blog for our updates related to how technology evolves, including Ubuntu, Windows, and others. Since then, we've extended our blog to include phones, tablets, our projects, and much more, which turned it from a small blog to a large tech blog that covers many topics related to technology.

During seven years, Aptivi Blog has gained recognition around tech communities, including social media sites (Twitter/X, Mastodon, Bluesky, and Tumblr) and the Fediverse, which gave us access to a larger tech audience around the whole world.

Fast forward to today, we are very excited to announce that the Aptivi Blog has now reached more than 100,000 all-time viewers, thanks to all our readers and our subscribers who interacted with our blog and its posts!

This is a huge milestone that we've managed to reach after more than seven years of our blog's lifetime. The last time we've announced a big milestone for our blog, it was when we had reached 50,000 all-time views on [May 1st, 2025](https://officialaptivi.wordpress.com/2025/05/01/a-new-milestone-50000-views/). We had celebrated this milestone by doing the following:

* Release a special version of our branding music exclusively on SoundCloud, The Mirage (Urban Gangsta House Remix), and a Nitrocid audio cue theme from this music starting from Nitrocid 0.1.2.10.
* Start the beta program for Terminaux 7.0 starting May 8th, 2025, for developers to test the upcoming version of Terminaux early.
* Re-introduce the library demonstration website later this year to allow users to demonstrate some parts of our libraries.
* Give the blog home page a makeover to list articles according to a specific category to find news of your preferred style faster.

After almost a year, we've now reached this milestone we've been waiting for! As a result, we will celebrate it with many exciting things that will happen, such as expanding our blog to include other tech-related topics.

Since 2019, Aptivi Blog has attained the following all-time views (2026 is excluded):

* 2019: 724 views, 330 visitors
* 2020: 4.1K views, 3.3K visitors
* 2021: 4K views, 3K visitors
* 2022: 4.1K views, 2.2K visitors
* 2023: 1.9K views, 1.3K visitors
* 2024: 17.2K views, 12.2K visitors
* 2025: 54K views, 38.6K visitors

As a way to appreciate our milestone that we've achieved, we'd like to thank all our readers for interacting with our blog, and we can't wait to see you in anothee huge milestone, which is 250,000 all-time views!

You'll witness the most exciting changes that will happen in both inside our blog and outside, so stay tuned for updates!
