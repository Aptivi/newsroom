Starting today, we are opening a dedicated newsroom for our project announcements and press releases. As we are working on our projects and other Aptivi assets, such as branding and other things, this newsroom will be updated to include new articles that talk about those updates.

We are very excited to announce that Aptivi Newsroom is now available as of today, with the following categories:

* Presses: This category contains press releases, which usually talk about new releases of our projects.
* Statuses: This category contains articles that talk about updates to project status.
* Others: This category contains other updates for Aptivi and projects.

As part of our commitment to ensuring that we provide the best of our projects, we are setting up this newsroom to ensure that we centralize all Aptivi-related announcements and updates to one unified place, separating them from the Aptivi Blog that used to host the same articles.

Aptivi Blog also contains articles that talk about our selective pressing for what's happening outside Aptivi, including updates related to Linux and Windows. It also contains articles that talk about our experiments.

The newsroom may seem to add complexity, but we are instead making our press releases more prominent and easier to distinguish from other articles, even though we're categorizing those articles in a dedicated category.

The newsroom is now publicly available, and is built with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/). Articles will be published when updates come, which may not mean every day. However, Aptivi Blog will re-publish those articles in a summarized form with links to the newsroom to ensure that we bring users to a dedicated place for more information, in case curiosity strikes.
