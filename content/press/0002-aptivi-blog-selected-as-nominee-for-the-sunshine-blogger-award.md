Aptivi Blog has started on March 1st, 2019, as the only resource for the latest updates related to Aptivi, its projects, and its updates, including new releases and other press releases. We are always determined to keep our blog up to date with the latest technical news that get our attention and are newsworthy.

Our useful content, along with the rejuvenation of our design that happened on February 12th and the freshness of our content, has always received praises, but we appreciate constructive criticism and we process all feedback. We are proud of this achievement.

As a result, we are very excited to announce that Aptivi Blog has been selected as a nominee for a community award for bloggers, called "**The Sunshine Blogger Award**"!

![The Sunshine Blogger Award](/newsroom/images/tsba.png)

If you haven't heard of this award, it's a community-backed award that lets bloggers nominate other bloggers for their useful and engaging content, with eleven answers, eleven questiins, and up to eleven bloggers to nominate.

The WordPress article about this award in our blog contains an exclusive message from Aptivi's operator written in first person point of view, which shows appreciation for this milestone!

We'd like to thank all readers who read the Aptivi Blog!
