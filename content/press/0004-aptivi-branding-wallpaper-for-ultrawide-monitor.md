title = 'Aptivi branding wallpapers now available for ultrawide monitors'

In [February 12th, 2026](https://officialaptivi.wordpress.com/2026/02/12/the-dawn-of-the-new-aptivi-branding-a-huge-milestone/), the renewed and reimagined Aptivi branding has been released, which includes our official branding assets for our logo, which represents the "Prism of the Tech Future" triangle that describes our adaptation to the future of technology moving forward. We know how fast technology evolves, and we have to keep up with it.

The Aptivi branding kit, which can be downloaded at the bottom of this article, contains our official assets for our branding, including our logo, our SVG graphics, and our official branding wallpaper, called "Modern". The wallpaper files, which start with `Modern-WP-`, are available in plain black, dark purple, and black-purple gradient backgrounds, and the following aspect ratios, with their minimum base resolutions, are supported:

| Aspect ratio | Base resolution |
|:-------------|:----------------|
| 16:9         | 1920x1080       |
|              | 3840x2160       |
|              | 7680x4320       |
| 16:10        | 1600x900        |
| 4:3          | 1024x768        |
| 8:5          | 2560x1600       |

Those wallpaper files are available in both the PNG format and the scalable vector graphics (SVG) format to support all use cases, including setting them as a desktop wallpaper.

We are very excited to announce that as of today, the Aptivi branding wallpapers are now available for ultra-wide monitors! The following aspect ratios were taken into account in the distribution of the official Aptivi wallpapers:

| Aspect ratio | Base resolution |
|:-------------|:----------------|
| 12:5         | 4320x1800       |
| 43:18        | 3440x1440       |
| 64:27        | 5120x2160       |

This should cover most ultra-wide monitors, including such monitors that are designed for gaming, as the AMOLED-friendly wallpaper variant is available with plain black background. As always, the source files are available in Affinity Studio v3 format, which you can download [here](https://www.affinity.studio/).
