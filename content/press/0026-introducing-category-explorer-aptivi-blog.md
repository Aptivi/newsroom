The second stage of our official Aptivi Blog's redesign, which we have talked about earlier when we finished redesigning it to provide a better user experience, has reached its conclusion. We have successfully managed to categorize all our blog articles as appropriate from the structure that we've created for our blog readers.

The Category Explorer is a simple page that allows you to take a look at all the categories that our official Aptivi Blog provides you, and the sidebar in other pages will only show you the broadest of the categories for simplified user experience, as future category updates will involve adding new categories, which will become bigger as we present you with more tech news.

Our categorization of the Aptivi Blog starts with:

* Aptivi
* Programming
* Technology
* Weekly and Monthly Updates

This gives you an impression that we are interested in pressing news about Aptivi and our projects, plus updates and press releases that are relayed from the Aptivi Newsroom, such as this one. Alongside those are programming news, general technology news, and weekly and monthly updates. However, those four categories are master categories, which means that so many articles have been posted in those categories during the lifetime of the Aptivi Blog.

Now, we'll explore deeper and tell you an example of how we've categorized our blog articles further in the official blog, and we'll choose Aptivi as the category. This one contains the following subcategories:

* Acknowledgements
* Projects
  * ADT
  * BassBoom
  * Libraries
  * MediaBoom
  * Nitrocid
  * Others
  * Terminaux
* Updates
* Website

The acknowledgements section shows how many awards we've collected, including "The Sunshine Blogger Award" that is run by the awesome WordPress community, and recognitions we've acknowledged. Also, we provide updates for our projects, including the Aptivi Development Toolkit (ADT), BassBoom, Nitrocid, Terminaux, and others. We also provide updates for our projects and other things that are related to Aptivi and its assets, as well as updates regarding the whole Aptivi website.

The Category Explorer shows you much more, and you can access it [here](https://officialaptivi.wordpress.com/category-explorer/).
