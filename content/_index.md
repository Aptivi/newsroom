## About Aptivi

Aptivi was established as an internet community on 2014 when we started providing the best solutions and repairs to our fellow computer users. We started from the computer forum websites as a member to a big community, and our missions have been expanded to provide you the best computer and mobile device solutions, as well as our tips and tricks and our programming projects. Today, we are providing you the best of the projects, made with love, care, and thought. We are focused on technology, and we are all about it, where tech changes. Our years of work have always helped users with the most complicated problems, while providing fresh news about our projects and about tech in general.
